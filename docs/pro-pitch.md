## Saaksh Pro
### Your multi-framework compliance copilot. *Your expertise, our build speed.*

The free Saaksh tool gets you a gap-analysed BRSR readiness plan in minutes. **Pro is
for when you move from preparing to actually doing the work** — collecting the data,
crunching the numbers, and producing something that survives a client's review and a
verifier's scrutiny. It does the spreadsheet archaeology so you keep the advisory layer.

---

### What you get

- **The compliance importer** *(coming — the headline)* — point it at the client's
  existing documents (last year's BRSR, the annual report, the energy audit) and it
  pre-fills the numbers into the right fields, each with the **source sentence shown**,
  for you to verify. It drafts the data entry; you check it. It never invents a figure.

- **Collect — chase the data without chasing people** *(live)* — request data from the
  client's team with branded emails and **automatic reminders**; they submit through a
  no-login form with evidence attached; emissions are **auto-computed and attributed**
  (which number came from whom, via which cited factor); and you get a draft of the
  responses.

- **Multi-framework mapping & export** *(BRSR live; cross-framework expanding)* — one set
  of client data, mapped across **BRSR ↔ GRI ↔ GHG ↔ CBAM ↔ TNFD ↔ IFRS**. Collect once,
  report many. This is where the repeated, manual translation work disappears.

- **Template & knowledge library** *(building)* — materiality formats, report skeletons,
  and "how to actually answer this" guidance (starting with the hard ones, like
  Principle 5 / Human Rights). The stuff you currently hunt for at 11pm.

---

### Why you can put your name on the output
Everything is **cited** (SEBI, ICAI, CEA, IPCC, with the source on every line) and
**evidence-backed** (the supporting bills/invoices attach to the figures, for
assurance). Where AI helps — explaining a field, drafting narrative, pre-filling from a
document — **it drafts and you verify; it never computes or invents a number.** Every
figure is either entered by a human or computed by a cited formula. That's what makes it
defensible in front of your client and their auditor.

---

### Who it's for, and how it works
- Built for **independent consultants and small firms** — not a bloated enterprise
  system that needs an admin to run it.
- **Early access, priced per engagement** — you pay per client you take on, not a
  year-round fee for seasonal work. (BRSR season is BRSR season; you shouldn't pay in
  the off-months.)
- It's early, and it's shaped by feedback from consultants like you — that's the point of
  bringing you in now.
